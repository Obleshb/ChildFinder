How It Works

Step 1: Register & Report

Create an account and submit a missing child report with recent photographs and detailed information about the child.

Step 2: AI Processing

Our advanced facial recognition system processes the images and creates a unique digital signature for identification.

Step 3: Database Matching

The system continuously scans and compares uploaded photos against our database of reported cases.

Step 4: Authority Action

When matches are found, authorities are immediately notified and begin investigation procedures.
