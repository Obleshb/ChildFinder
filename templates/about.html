Frequently Asked Questions

Our system uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze facial features and match them against our database of reported missing children. The technology considers multiple facial points and can account for age progression and different angles.

First, register an account on our platform. Once registered, you can submit a report with the child's details and recent photographs. The case will be immediately visible to registered law enforcement agencies and will be added to our database for automated matching.

We employ industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect all data. Access to sensitive information is strictly controlled and only available to verified law enforcement authorities. All data handling complies with relevant privacy laws and regulations.

When our system identifies a potential match, it immediately notifies relevant authorities who will verify the match and take appropriate action. The reporting family will be contacted through official channels once the match is confirmed by authorities.
